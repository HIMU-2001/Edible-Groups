About Us

Quality Edible Oils and Seeds: Nurturing Health and Trust for Over Three Decades


For over three decades, Edible Group has been a trusted name in the edible oil industry, delivering a diverse range of premium oils and oil seeds to meet the ever-evolving needs of our cherished customers. We take immense pride in our journey, which has been defined by unwavering dedication to quality, relentless innovation, and a deep-rooted understanding of our customers' preferences.

At Edible Group, we believe that the true essence of cooking lies in the ingredients we use. With this belief, we have honed our craft to produce oils that not only elevate the taste of your culinary creations but also contribute to your overall well-being. Our mission is to bring nature's finest offerings to your kitchen, empowering you to create remarkable flavors and lead a healthy lifestyle.

Mission and Vision

Aiming to be a world-class manufacturer of affordable quality products, we prioritize innovation, sustainability, and ethical business practices to create value for stakeholders.

1. Mission

To improve competitiveness through innovations, cost control & to enhance the market share in domestic as well as international markets. To produce efficiently and economically in an Eco-friendly manner with due regard to safety, conservation and quality. To continue to diversify and grow, addressing new products and markets..

2. Vision

To be a world class manufacture of quality products at affordable prices with focus on state-of-the-art facilities to maintain sustained long term growth, and to create value for all stake holders.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Creating Lasting Impact: Empowering Communities, Nurturing the Planet.


At Edible Group, we believe that business success goes hand in hand with making a positive impact on society and the environment. We are deeply committed to fulfilling our corporate social responsibility by implementing initiatives that drive meaningful change and contribute to the well-being of communities around us. Our CSR efforts are guided by the principles of sustainability, education, healthcare, and environmental conservation.
