Kanchan Oil Industries Ltd.

  1. Home
  2. Details

MANUFACTURERS AND REFINERS OF ALL KINDS OF EDIBLE OILS.

Kanchan Oil Industries Limited, is a medium scale industry situated at Jhargram, West Bengal, was (a sick unit) taken over in the year 1988 from Government of West Bengal (WBFC & UBI.) The plant is situated 0.5 Km away from Railway Station and 170 KM from Kolkata . Original activity of the company was extraction of oil through solvent process & refining. In the year 1995, the company extended its activity and started CPO refining & hydrogenation of Oil. The company is an ISO 9001-2000 certified organization and had been awarded by GLOBE OIL INDIA as emerging Company for the year 2007. Presently KOIL is ISO, Agmark and VOP certified company. It specializes in manufacturing of edible oil and fractionated oil. Votator has been installed for production of specialty products like Bakery Shortening and Margarine. All products are thoroughly tested in our in-house laboratory under the supervision of highly qualified and experienced Chemists.
