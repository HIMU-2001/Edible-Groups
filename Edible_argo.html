EAPL IS A LEADING MANUFACTURER & EXPORTER OF PREMIUM QUALITY SESAME OIL, SESAME SEEDS, RICE BRAN OIL, EDIBLE OILS & ITS ALLIED PRODUCTS.

Established in 2003, with an Expeller Unit of Sesame Oil, today it is equipped with state of the art facilities for sorting, cleaning, crushing and processing of sesame seeds ,roasted sesame oil & natural sesame oil. In 2015 a new Unit with Buhler Plant of Germany was set up for cleaning of seeds with quality improvement & efficiency. EAPL has its unique presence in the center of major sesame & rice cultivating areas of West Bengal & eastern parts of India with the experience of over three decades in edible oil and oil seeds and ever growing market of quality products. We have taken a leap and made our products available off shore. Our endeavor is to make these products available at all the major international markets .

Year Of Establishment : 2003

Domestic & Global Market : Our high level of consistency in terms of products & service quality has helped us to establish a strong market goodwill & wide client base. We aspire to achieve long term business relationship with our clients based on strong corporate ethics. Edible Agro Products Limited has now become a leading manufacturer and exporter of best quality Sesame Oil and other allied products. We are relentlessly serving our customers with the purest Sesame Oil & best quality Sesame Seeds. We are fast growing,with our client base increasing,both globally and domestically.The export of sesame oils and sesame seed were started in 2006 and it grew year after year. Presently it has presence in the international markets of China, Taiwan, Vietnam, Mexico, UAE, Germany, Italy, Natherland, Srilanka, Singapore, Malaysia and is targeting to breakthrough European countries as well USA.

Testing & Quality Check: Since its inception, have grown from strength to strength to become one of the leading group involved in Manufacturing & Export of premium quality edible oils in Eastern India. Why Our Products ?

  • Seeds are directly procured from farmers, cultivated on top quality soil & stored in a controlled environment.
  • Seeds are graded on the basis of FFA & moisture.
  • Best quality seeds are first cleaned by multilayer cleaning system.
  • The cleaning process eliminates all dry leaves & immature seeds to obtain quality oil.
  • Processed seeds are carefully pressed without any chemical solvent or Preservatives,ensuring that oil retains its natural aroma & colour.
  • Seeds & extracted Oil is sold all across the global market.
  • Each consignment carries a quality certificate.
  • Regular health check-up of the workers ensures hygienic environment.
  • We are accredited with the ISO 9000, HACCP, SQF, GMP, GHP certificates.
